About Mproteins

M/s Mproteins Established in the year 2017, We are growing rapidly due to our ability of processing, supplying, wholesaling, exporting and trading a qualitative and comprehensive range of agro products, Whole Spices, Spices Blends, Powdered Spices, Cereals, Pulses , Natural oil extracts, Instant Spice Mix Powder branded in the name of Shiv spices. The products consist of in these categories are Mushrooms, Red Chilies, Pepper, Turmeric, Coriander, Nut meg, Cinnamon, Chick Peas, and many more. Processed under hygienic and disinfected environment, our complete product range is admired among the clients for their high nutritional value, purity, freshness, taste and strong aroma . We ensure that our products are in line with the quality parameters and specified standards of Food and Beverage industry. Transparency and accuracy in all our business operations have made us a genuine as well as authorized name in the market. From the day one of our establishment, we are consistently moving ahead to compete with the different market challenges.
